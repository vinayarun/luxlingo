# LuxLingo Curriculum Design: CEFR Level A1 (Resident Expat)

## 🎯 Objective
To guide a resident expat to a **solid A1 proficiency** (~450 words).
This curriculum goes beyond "survival" to include vocabulary needed for daily life in Luxembourg.

**Target**: 20 Units x ~25 words = ~500 words.

---

## 📚 Curriculum Structure (20 Units)

### Phase 1: Survival (Units 1-5)
*Focus: Immediate needs, basic interactions.*
*Status: Implemented (to be densified)*

1.  **Basics**: Greetings, Introductions, Verbs *sinn/heeschen*.
2.  **Essentials**: Numbers 0-10, Colors, Objects, Gender *den/d'/dat*.
3.  **Food & Drink**: Ordering, Café, Plurals.
4.  **Getting Around**: Transport, Directions, Prepositions.
5.  **Shopping & Help**: Supermarket, Clothes, Emergency, Adjectives.

### Phase 2: Settling In (Units 6-10)
*Focus: Personal life, home, and routine.*

6.  **Family & People**: Family members, Friends, Possessives (*mäin/däin*).
7.  **Home & Living**: Rooms, Furniture, Renting, *Wunnen*.
8.  **Time & Calendar**: Days, Months, Time, Appointments.
9.  **Work & Daily Routine**: Jobs, Verbs *schaffen/maachen*, Simple Present.
10. **Weather & Seasons**: Weather, Seasons, Clothing II.

### Phase 3: Vocabulary Expansion (Units 11-15)
*Focus: Broadening vocabulary for specific contexts.*

11. **The Body & Health**: Body parts, Doctor visits, Feelings.
12. **Nature & Environment**: Animals, Landscapes, Plants.
13. **City Life**: Post office, Bank, Administration, Places.
14. **Food II (Cooking)**: Ingredients, Recipes, Kitchen verbs.
15. **Clothes & Fashion II**: Accessories, Materials, Shopping dialogues.

### Phase 4: Integration (Units 16-20)
*Focus: Socializing, culture, and complex sentences.*

16. **Hobbies & Sports**: Activities, *Gär hunn* (to like).
17. **Travel & Geography**: Countries, Languages, Holidays.
18. **Media & Technology**: Phone, Computer, Internet.
19. **Celebrations**: National Day, Christmas, Traditions.
20. **Review & Consolidation**: Mixed exercises covering all A1 topics.

---

## 🔬 Scientific Approach: The Hybrid Model

### 1. High Density Functional Units (1-10)
*   **Goal**: Grammar + Core Vocabulary.
*   **Density**: Increase from ~15 to **25 words/unit**.
*   **Method**: Teach words in functional sentences (e.g., instead of just "Apple", teach "I eat a red apple").

### 2. Thematic Expansion Units (11-20)
*   **Goal**: Bulk Vocabulary Acquisition.
*   **Density**: **30+ words/unit**.
*   **Method**: "Vocabulary Builder" style.
    *   Focus on **Recall** (Image -> Word).
    *   Focus on **Categorization** (Fruit vs Vegetable).
    *   Less focus on new grammar, more on reinforcing existing patterns with new words.

### 3. Spaced Repetition
*   Words from Phase 1 (e.g., Colors) must reappear in Phase 3 (e.g., "Green trees", "Red blood").
